Ghaziabad: 13 killed in fire at illegal garment factory


Thirteen employees of an illegal garment factory suffocated and burnt to death after a fire broke out in Ghaziabad’s Shaheed Nagar area early Friday morning. Three others sustained injuries in the incident and are undergoing treatment at Guru Teg Bahadur (GTB) Hospital in the capital, said police.
According to the police, the incident took place around 5 am on Friday at a jacket manufacturing unit operating from a three-storey building located in Sahibabad.
